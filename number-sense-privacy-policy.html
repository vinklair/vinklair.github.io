Privacy Policy for Number Sense - Hearing

Last Updated: December 13, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Number Sense - Hearing (“the App”) treats your personal information and data. By using the App, you agree to the terms and practices described below.

1. No Collection of Personal Data

Number Sense - Hearing does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. You can use all features of the App without providing personal details such as your name, email address, or contact information.

2. No Usage Analytics or Tracking

The App does not include any analytics frameworks, tracking tools, or advertising networks. Your interactions and activity within the App remain on your device and are not monitored or recorded by the App’s developers.

3. No Third-Party Access

Since the App does not collect or store any user data, there is no data to share or sell to third parties. We do not disclose any information - personal or otherwise - to third parties, including advertisers, marketing partners, or analytics providers.

4. Data on Your Device

All settings, preferences, and progress (such as your practice streaks and language selections) are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device unless you choose to share it manually (for example, through a screenshot or device backup).

5. Security

While no personal data is collected, we maintain standard security measures to ensure the App remains safe and free of vulnerabilities. Regular updates help keep the App stable and secure.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App’s practices, please contact us at vinklair.apps@gmail.com

By using Number Sense - Hearing, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to be bound by its terms. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the App.
