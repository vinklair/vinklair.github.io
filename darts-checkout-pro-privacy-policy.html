Privacy Policy for Darts Checkout Pro App

Last Updated: March 28, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Darts Checkout Pro app (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Non-Personal Data

Usage and Diagnostics Information: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect data on how you interact with the App. This includes which features you use, crash reports, and performance metrics. This information helps improve the App’s functionality and stability.

b. Local Data Storage

Game Scores and Settings: Any results from subtraction and addition games, as well as user preferences, are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This information is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

c. In-App Purchase Information

Transaction Data: When you purchase the App, Apple processes your payment information. We receive transaction confirmation but do not access your credit card number or other sensitive financial details.

2. How We Use the Information

Analytics and Improvements: Non-personal usage and diagnostic data are used to understand user behavior, fix bugs, improve performance, and enhance user experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share non-personal information with analytics providers:

  • Firebase (by Google): For analytics and crash reporting.

Please consult their privacy policy for details:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We take commercially reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no security measure can be guaranteed to be 100% effective.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete the data.

6. Your Choices

Analytics Opt-Out: Currently, we do not offer a direct opt-out for analytics and crash reporting. You may refrain from using the App if you are uncomfortable with these measures.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy upon changes. Continued use of the App indicates your acceptance of these updates.

8. Contact Us
